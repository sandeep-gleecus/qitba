WHY ENTER?

Be part of the Qatar IT Business Awards 2018 and reap the rewards for your organisation.

The awards presentation will be held on the 6th of May under the patronage of HE The Minister of Transport and Communications.

As one of the most prestigious awards for the technology sector in Qatar, you will gain the recognition you deserve from peers and stakeholders. In addition, there are several advantages to participating in the event.

  • Gain valuable exposure for your company and achievements
  • Showcase your company’s expertise and positive role in the development of IT in Qatar
  • Reward your partners, staff and shareholders for a job well done
  • Attract industry talents by establishing your company as one of the industry’s best.
  • Benchmark your company’s achievements against the best in the industry
  • Network with leading industry and Government leaders during the gala event
