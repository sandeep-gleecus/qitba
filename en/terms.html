General Terms and Conditions

  • Categories are open to only Qatari-founded companies. Submissions from organizations founded abroad will not be accepted. The five categories for smart solutions will be exempted from this condition.
  • Submissions should be based on achievements made during 2017 only
  • Only one winner for each category will be awarded
  • One company can apply in all categories but cannot win in more than one category
  • Any missing mandatory information will result in rejection and rejections will not be informed during the evaluation process
  • Reaching out to any judges will be considered a disqualification
  • All material submitted will remain confidential
  • The judges reserve the right to amend category selections where appropriate
  • The judges reserve the right to disqualify any entry not accompanied by the appropriate supporting documentation
  • The judges' decision will be final and no correspondence will be entered into before or after the judging
  • No feedback on the submissions will be provided
  • Entries not made through the on-line submission system will not be accepted
  • The winners of each category will be announced at the presentation of the Awards; under no circumstances will the details be disclosed before that time
  • Closing date for entries is 22th April 2018, and entries received after the final closing date will not be accepted
